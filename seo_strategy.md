- Public marketing pages in `artifacts/flight-school`
- Programmatic landing pages and air-tour pages generated from sitemap-driven prerendering
- Shared SEO infrastructure (`index.html`, `Seo.tsx`, `SeoSchema.tsx`, `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated/admin pages under `/admin/**`
